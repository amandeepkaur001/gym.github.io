Fitness First





MARINA BAY
FINANCIAL CENTRE





Fitness First is an international fitness centre brand founded in 1993 in the United Kingdom. It was later introduced into Singapore around 2001 and now there are 19 clubs all over Singapore. The Marina Bay Financial Centre (MBFC) venue is one of the most convenient locations for working adults as it is right smack in the middle of Central Business District (CBD) area. The working adults are able to get in their exercise before, in-between or after work as Fitness First provides fitness solutions to busy executives around Marina Bay Financial Centre with this 13,003 square feet club.

Marina Bay Financial Centre itself is an award-winning grade-A development which clinched top awards in the FIABCI Prix d'Excellence Awards 2012 (Office category) that recognises the world's outstanding real estate developments. Tower 3, where the club is located, is easily accessible via the Marina Bay MRT (Circle Line) and Downtown MRT (Downtown Line) and East Coast Parkway expressway.
