Customisable Programmes

Classes

Here at Fitness First, we provide many different types of classes:

- Feature Class

As we cater mainly to the working public in the MBFC area at this outlet, we have customisable programmes for everyone. Class Booker App

Planning ahead or squeezing in a last-minute class? Class Booker lets you find out and book whatever is available in an instant. 

Found a class that you would love to be a regular at? The app also automatically saves your selections, so it’s easy to check the schedule whenever you’d like.

Class Booker allows you to book available classes in any of our clubs island-wide. Please speak to our friendly staff for any assistance. 

To avoid any disappointments, do download the Fitness First Class Booker app now, for free. It is available both on Google Play and the App Store. The app makes booking a Group Exercise class simple, convenient and fast!!
