Special Features

The MBFC Fitness First outlet has many edges over the other outlets.

1) This outlet's floor-to-ceiling windows overlook the Marina Bay financial district, offering a breathtaking view to members working out at the free-weight area and cardio area.

2) Across all 20 outlets, MBFC Fitness First is the only outlet that offers unique Tour De France bikes for its Procycling Triathlon Training class to serve peak performance.

3) It has a large area on the gym floor set aside for functional training, complete with freestyle equipment. In addition, members can reap the benefits of cutting-edge functional / Freestyle Movement Training led by qualified Freestyle Specialist trainers by participating in Group Suspension Training and BOSU® classes in its multi-purpose studio. 

Enjoy the collage of the MBFC district and the skyline view that you will be able to experience from the MBFC Fitness First outlet!
